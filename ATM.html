All About ATM

What is ATM?

ATM stands for Applied Technology Magnet which is a 3 year computer program at Bancroft Middle School. In ATM students learn different programs on the computers and get to learn more about them and explore them.

In 6th grade ATM the main focus is on being safe on the internet and typing which is a must need role for working on and with the computer.

In 7th grade I learned how to use computer aided drafting and graphics.

In 8th grade I learned how to make a website go live and code.
